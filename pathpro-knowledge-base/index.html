What is PathPro?

PathPro is a product development operating system designed for product teams, indie makers, and SaaS founders. It replaces the scattered collection of tools you use today — voting boards, project trackers, changelog generators, and feedback forms — with a single, unified workspace.

With PathPro, your community can vote on features, submit feedback, report bugs, and create support tickets. Your team can manage roadmaps, track tasks, and publish release notes — all from one platform that lives on your own subdomain or custom domain.

Use Case Shout-Out
SaaS founders use PathPro to replace 4+ separate tools — one workspace for voting, roadmaps, changelogs, and support.

How It Works

PathPro follows a core workflow loop that keeps your community engaged and your product moving forward:

  1. Community votes — Your users propose and upvote the features they want most.
  2. You prioritize — Use voting data, AI-powered demand scoring, and community submissions to decide what to build next.
  3. Team builds — Manage your roadmap with task groups, tasks, subtasks, and assignments.
  4. Release notes close the loop — Publish what you shipped and notify the people who asked for it.

This cycle repeats continuously, creating a transparent relationship between you and your community that builds trust, reduces churn, and ensures you're always building the right thing.

Who Is It For?

  • SaaS product teams who want to centralize feedback and roadmap planning.
  • Indie makers who need a lightweight way to track community requests.
  • Open-source maintainers looking for a public voting and roadmap tool.
  • Agency teams managing multiple client projects with separate communities.
