Frequently asked questions

What does the Overview dashboard show?

The Overview dashboard gives you a real-time snapshot of your product: open tasks, community submissions, member growth, support tickets, and release history — all in one view.

How is customer sentiment analyzed?

PathPro’s AI analyzes the tone of every comment, submission, and conversation to score sentiment as positive, neutral, or negative. Results are aggregated into trends you can track over time.

Can I track team performance and sprint health?

Yes. PathPro tracks task completion rates, team activity, and sprint velocity. Color-coded health indicators help you spot bottlenecks and keep delivery on track.

Can I export analytics data?

Absolutely. You can export reports as PDF or CSV, share read-only dashboard links, and schedule recurring reports to stakeholders.

How does PathFox predict trends?

PathFox analyzes historical patterns in growth, feedback, and engagement data to forecast future trends, detect anomalies, and surface actionable recommendations.

Is the analytics dashboard included in the free plan?

Yes. The Overview dashboard, growth tracking, and basic analytics are included in the free plan. Advanced AI-powered insights are available on paid plans.
