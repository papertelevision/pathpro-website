Frequently asked questions

How does feature voting work?

Community members browse your feature list and upvote the ideas they care about most. Each vote is counted in real time, giving your team a clear signal of demand.

Can anyone submit a feature request?

Yes. Any registered community member can submit new feature requests, report bugs, or suggest improvements. Submissions are visible to your team immediately.

How are demand scores calculated?

PathFox analyzes vote counts, comment activity, sentiment patterns, and engagement trends to generate a demand score for each feature.

Can I control which features are visible to the public?

Absolutely. You choose which features appear on your public voting board. Internal ideas stay private.

Do voters get notified when a feature ships?

Yes. When you ship a feature, voters and commenters are notified through release notes, closing the feedback loop.

Is feature voting included in the free plan?

Yes. Feature voting, community submissions, and unlimited community members are included in the free plan. No credit card required.
