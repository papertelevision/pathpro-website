Meet Our Product Lead

Josh Sears

Founder & Product Lead

Josh co-founded his first tech company in 2007 and went on to establish PaperTelevision LLC, a creative technology studio that delivered digital products for clients including Whirlpool, Tony Hawk Foundation, and the University of Notre Dame.

In 2016, he built MapifyPro — a mapping solution for WordPress — and scaled it into one of the leading products in its category. He guided the product through a successful acquisition in 2020, learning first-hand what it takes to build, grow, and support a SaaS product that customers depend on.

Josh created PathPro to solve the problems he experienced across 15+ years of product development: scattered feedback, disconnected tools, and no single place to plan, build, ship, and support a product. PathPro is the platform he always wished existed.
