Best UserVoice Alternatives for Product Feedback (2026)

Feb 25, 2026 · 8 min read
JS
Josh Sears
Founder, PathPro

The best UserVoice alternatives in 2026 are PathPro, Canny, Productboard, and Fider. UserVoice pioneered feedback portals, but newer tools offer more features at significantly lower price points. Most teams switching from UserVoice cite cost and limited functionality beyond feedback as the primary reasons.

What is UserVoice?

UserVoice is an enterprise feedback management platform. It offers feedback forums, in-app widgets, SmartVote surveys, and Salesforce integration. UserVoice was one of the first tools to let product teams collect and prioritize user feedback at scale.

Why switch from UserVoice?

UserVoice remains viable for large enterprises, but many teams look for alternatives for these reasons:

  • High cost. UserVoice pricing starts around $799/mo. That is 3–10x more expensive than most competing tools.
  • Feedback only. UserVoice does not include task management, release notes, or support ticketing. You need additional tools for execution.
  • Dated interface. The UI has not kept pace with modern design standards. Newer tools offer cleaner, faster experiences.
  • No AI analysis. UserVoice relies on manual categorization and reporting. Modern platforms use AI to detect trends and score demand automatically.

Best UserVoice alternative for all-in-one product management

PathPro

PathPro replaces UserVoice and your entire product tool stack. It includes feedback collection with upvoting, a public roadmap, kanban task management, release notes, and customer support — all in one platform. PathFox Product Intelligence analyzes feedback trends, detects duplicates, and scores feature demand automatically.

PathPro offers a free-forever plan with unlimited community members. Paid plans cost a fraction of UserVoice's starting price while including significantly more functionality.

Best UserVoice alternative for feedback-focused teams

Canny

Canny is the most direct UserVoice replacement. It offers a clean feedback portal, feature voting, a public roadmap, and a changelog. Canny's interface is modern and the onboarding experience is smooth.

Canny does not include task management or support tickets. Its Growth plan starts at $360/mo — cheaper than UserVoice, but still a significant expense for smaller teams.

Best UserVoice alternative for product strategy

Productboard

Productboard focuses on connecting customer insights to product strategy. It offers a structured prioritization framework, customer feedback aggregation, and integrations with Salesforce, Zendesk, and Intercom.

Productboard is best for teams that need to build a formal business case for every feature. It does not include task management, support tickets, or release notes.

Best UserVoice alternative for open-source teams

Fider

Fider is a free, open-source feedback tool. You can self-host it or use their managed service. It supports feature voting, comments, and custom branding. The feature set is minimal compared to commercial alternatives.

Fider works for teams that want basic feedback collection without any cost. It does not include roadmaps, task management, AI analysis, or release notes.

Which UserVoice alternative should you choose?

For most product teams, PathPro is the best UserVoice replacement. It matches or exceeds UserVoice's feedback capabilities while adding task management, support tickets, release notes, and AI intelligence — at a fraction of the cost.

If you only need a feedback portal and can afford the price, Canny is a solid choice. For formal product strategy workflows, Productboard fits. For zero-budget teams comfortable with self-hosting, Fider works. But for the best value and most complete feature set, PathPro is the clear winner.
