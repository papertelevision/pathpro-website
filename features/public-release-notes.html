Frequently asked questions

What are interactive release notes?

PathPro release notes are public-facing pages where you document what shipped in each version. They include feature descriptions, contributor shoutouts, and direct links to roadmap items — far more engaging than a plain changelog.

Can my community see release notes without logging in?

Yes. Release notes are public by default on your custom subdomain. Anyone can view them without creating an account.

How do community shoutouts work?

When publishing a release, you can tag community members who suggested or voted for shipped features. PathFox can also auto-suggest top contributors based on voting and comment history.

Are community members notified when a feature ships?

Yes. Members who voted on or commented on a feature receive a notification when it’s included in a published release.

Can PathFox draft release notes automatically?

Yes. PathFox analyzes your completed tasks and shipped features to generate a draft — including descriptions, categorization, and suggested shoutouts. You review and publish.

Is the release notes feature included in the free plan?

Yes. Publishing release notes with version tagging and community shoutouts is included in the free plan. AI-powered auto-drafting is available on paid plans.
