Widgets

Widgets are standalone, embeddable versions of each core project section. Instead of sending visitors to your full PathPro project, you can embed specific sections directly into your own website — a feedback page, a product updates page, a support portal, or anywhere else that makes sense for your workflow.

To access widget embed codes, go to Settings & Design → Widgets tab in your project. Each widget has customizable settings for width, height, margins, and background color. As you adjust these settings, the embed code updates in real time. When you're happy with the configuration, copy the code and paste it into any HTML page.

If you're not sure where to start, open the help (?) dropdown in your project header and look for the "How to Publish" guided tour. It walks you through the widget configuration process step by step.

Full Project Widget

The Full Project embed places your entire PathPro project interface inside a single iframe — header, navigation, and all public sections included. Visitors can browse your roadmap, vote on features, read release notes, submit support tickets, and more, all without leaving your website. This is the simplest way to publish your project: one embed code gives your audience the complete experience.

Unlike the individual widgets below, the Full Project embed doesn't include a background color setting because the project's own styling controls the appearance. You can still configure width, height, and margins from the Settings & Design → Widgets tab — it appears as the first card on the page.

Feature Voting Widget

The Feature Voting widget embeds your full voting board. Visitors can browse feature requests, upvote the ones they care about, and submit new ideas — all without leaving your website. This is ideal for feedback pages where you want to capture user input in the context of your own brand and site design.

Roadmap Widget

The Roadmap widget displays your public roadmap with task groups, statuses, and progress indicators. Visitors can see what you're working on, what's coming next, and what's been completed. Embed this on a product updates or "what's next" page to keep your audience informed about your development progress.

Release Notes Widget

The Release Notes widget shows your public changelog — the latest announcements, updates, and product changes. Embed this on a changelog or "what's new" page so visitors can stay up to date with your latest releases without navigating away from your site.

Support Widget

The Support widget embeds a support ticket form where visitors can submit tickets directly from your website. This is perfect for a help or contact page — visitors describe their issue and submit it without needing a separate support portal or email workflow.

Use Case Shout-Out
Embed the Feature Voting widget on your feedback page so customers can vote without leaving your site, and place the Roadmap widget on a product updates page so they can see what you're building next — all within your own brand experience.
