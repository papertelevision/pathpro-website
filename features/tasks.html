Frequently asked questions

What views are available for tasks?

PathPro supports kanban board and list views. Organize tasks into groups, drag them between columns, and switch views depending on how your team prefers to work.

Can I create subtasks?

Yes. Any task can have subtasks for breaking down complex work. Subtasks have their own assignees, statuses, and due dates while rolling up progress to the parent task.

How does team assignment work?

Assign any team member to a task or subtask. Team members see their assignments across all projects, and admins can view workload distribution at a glance.

Can community members see internal tasks?

No. Tasks are private to your team by default. Community members interact through feature voting and your public roadmap — they never see your internal task boards.

What is PathFox and how does it help with tasks?

PathFox is PathPro’s AI assistant. It analyzes your team’s work patterns, surfaces priority insights, summarizes discussions, and can even automate task creation based on community feedback.

Is task management included in the free plan?

PathPro’s free plan includes basic task management, but does not include advanced features such as file upload or team management.
